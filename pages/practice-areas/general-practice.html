General Practice

Dr. Carlson specializes in work with persons with adjustment difficulties, and who are experiencing stress and anxiety while making life transitions. He also is particularly interested in working with those who have culturally unpopular or unsupported life styles, and who experience marginalization or disempowerment. His practice is committed to helping people improve their well-being, alleviate stress and maladjustment, resolve crises, and increase their ability to function better in their lives.
